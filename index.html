WELCOME TO THE U.S. NUTRITION MEDICAL EDUCATION GRANT PROGRAM

Reckitt/Mead Johnson Nutrition grant funding is based on high-quality continuing education programs for healthcare professionals to provide infant and child nutrition for a healthy start to life.

Grant requests will be considered from U.S. institutions, professional organizations and associations.

Medical Education Grants should be submitted through this portal at a minimum of 90 days prior to the program date(s) to be considered.

Login above to submit a grant or check status. First time users, register here.

By applying to the Reckitt/MJN US Nutrition Medical Education Grant Program, you have read our privacy policy and understand how your data will be handled.

Questions related to the grant program, application process, or grant portal should be directed to meadjohnson.grants1@reckitt.com.
